---
title: LEDs, Status Indicators
description: The LEDs provide system status feedback on the Geostix.
---

GEOSTIX devices are equipped with **four status indicators**: three on the front panel and one on the rear side. The front-facing LEDs are multicolor and indicate the operating status of:
- the **GNSS module** (satellite icon)
- the **GEOSTIX microcontroller** (CPU icon)
- the **Bluetooth interface** (Bluetooth icon, bottom)



The rear-facing LED is **green-only** and indicates **battery charging activity**.
